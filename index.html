Everyone showed up prepared for the meeting and ready to get started on the project. Some of the suggested ideas were the following: Pomodoro Timer, To-Do List, or a Calculator.

One of the primary concerns introduced by the group was regarding the vague timeline requirements set forth by the project manager. King Triton was ultimately named the team leader and was tasked with asking the PM whether their produced timeline was reasonable.
